# ChatX - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is ChatX?
ChatX is an AI-powered learning platform that helps you understand and learn from any content. Upload PDFs, share YouTube links, or paste text, and our AI will help you learn through interactive conversations, quizzes, and flashcards.

### Is ChatX free to use?
ChatX offers both free and premium subscription tiers. Free users have access to basic features with usage limits. Premium subscribers get unlimited AI interactions, advanced features, and priority support.

### What platforms is ChatX available on?
ChatX is available as:
- Web application (https://chatx.verixence.com)
- Android mobile app (Google Play Store)
- iOS mobile app (coming soon)

## Account & Authentication

### How do I create an account?
1. Download the ChatX app or visit our website
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Start using ChatX!

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Can I use the same account on multiple devices?
Yes! Your ChatX account works across all platforms. Sign in with the same credentials on web, Android, or iOS to sync your content and chat history.

### How do I delete my account?
1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to Account Management
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion
**Note**: This permanently deletes all your data including content, chat history, and quizzes.

## Content & Features

### What types of content can I upload?
- **PDFs**: Research papers, textbooks, articles (up to 10MB)
- **YouTube Videos**: Any public YouTube video via URL
- **Text**: Copy and paste any text content

### How does the AI chat work?
Once you upload content, ChatX's AI analyzes it and can:
- Answer questions about the content
- Explain complex concepts
- Provide summaries
- Help with studying and comprehension

### What are Quizzes?
ChatX automatically generates multiple-choice quizzes based on your uploaded content to test your understanding. You can:
- Take quizzes anytime
- Review correct answers
- Track your scores
- Retake quizzes to improve

### What are Flashcards?
Flashcards are AI-generated study cards with:
- **Front**: Key terms or questions
- **Back**: Definitions or answers
Perfect for memorization and quick review sessions.

### Can I edit or delete my uploaded content?
Yes! Go to your Dashboard, find the content you want to manage, and tap the menu icon to edit or delete it.

### Is there a limit on content uploads?
- **Free users**: Limited number of uploads per month
- **Premium users**: Unlimited uploads
- **File size limit**: PDFs up to 10MB

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption:
- HTTPS for all data transmission
- Encrypted data storage
- Secure authentication
- No data sold to third parties

### Who can see my uploaded content?
Only you can see your content. We don't share your uploads with other users. Content is processed by AI services under strict privacy agreements.

### Do you use my content to train AI models?
No. Your content is used only to provide you with personalized responses and is not used to train AI models.

### How long is my data stored?
Your data remains stored as long as your account is active. When you delete content or your account, it's permanently removed from our servers (excluding legal backup requirements).

## Technical Support

### The app isn't working properly. What should I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Update to the latest app version
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Clear app cache (Settings > Clear Cache)
5. Contact support if issues persist

### I'm getting an error when uploading content. Why?
Common reasons:
- File too large (PDFs must be under 10MB)
- Unsupported file format
- Poor internet connection
- Server maintenance (check our status page)

### Can I use ChatX offline?
Limited offline access:
- View previously loaded content
- Review saved quizzes and flashcards
- Active AI chat requires internet connection

### Which AI models does ChatX use?
ChatX uses state-of-the-art language models including:
- OpenAI GPT models
- Groq (for faster responses)
- Custom optimizations for educational content

## Troubleshooting

### Why is the AI response slow?
- High server load (try again in a few minutes)
- Large content being processed
- Network connectivity issues
- Switch to Groq model for faster responses (Settings)

### My quiz scores aren't saving
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check internet connection
- Update to latest app version
- Contact support if problem persists

### YouTube video won't load
- Verify the video is public (not private/age-restricted)
- Check if video has captions/transcript available
- Some videos may not be supported due to copyright
